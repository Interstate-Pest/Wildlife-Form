GUARANTEE OF EXCLUSION SERVICES

Interstate Pest Management guarantees all exclusion services rendered for 1 Year. The guarantee may be extended each year after, pending completion of a $200 annual inspection of the home. Our Forever Warranty is offered to customers who subscribe to our ALL Seasons Plus reoccurring maintenance service. The guarantee covers the EXACT locations where exclusion services were performed by Interstate Pest Management, INC. (Referred to as "IPM"). "IPM" will not guarantee that an animal will never gain access but more so that access will not be obtained through exclusion work that "IPM" deemed necessary to exclude and performed said exclusion work. Under our guarantee, if an animal gains access in a specific area(s) that was excluded, "IPM" agrees to trap for up to 1 week and repair the exclusion work in the same area at no additional charge. If "IPM" is called out for a breach in the exclusion and it is determined that there was no breach in our exclusion work, then there will be a trip/inspection charge depending on travel time and time involved in the inspection of $125 per occurrence. Under no circumstances will "IPM" warranty or guarantee any work performed by other companies. If there is preexisting work done and "IPM" is hired to fix the work done, "IPM" only guarantees the areas where exclusion work was performed by "IPM." Any modifications (examples: remodeling, painting, storm damage) to our exclusion work will void our warranty & guarantee.
